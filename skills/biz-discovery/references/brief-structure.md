# Brief Structure

## Recommended structure

### 1. Background
What is happening now?

### 2. Problem / Opportunity
What problem needs solving, or what opportunity is being pursued?

### 3. Objective
What is the immediate objective?

### 4. Stakeholders
Who cares, who owns, who is affected?

### 5. Scope snapshot
What is likely in scope? What is likely out of scope?

### 6. Constraints
Budget, timeline, policy, tech, resource, organizational constraints

### 7. Assumptions and unknowns
Separate clearly

### 8. Risks / ambiguity points
What could derail later writing or execution?

### 9. Recommended next doc
What should be written next and why?
