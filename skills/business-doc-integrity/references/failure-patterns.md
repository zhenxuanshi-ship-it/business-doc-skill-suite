# Common Failure Patterns

## 1. Recommendation leap
The document jumps to a recommendation without enough comparative basis.

## 2. Scope drift
Early scope is smaller than later commitments, or non-scope items quietly return.

## 3. Naming drift
The same module, team, system, or phase gets renamed without explanation.

## 4. Timeline fantasy
Milestones look clean on paper but ignore dependencies and sequencing.

## 5. Risk theater
Risks are listed, but mitigations are generic and non-operational.

## 6. Budget mismatch
Numbers differ between summary, body, and appendices or tables.

## 7. Architecture-document mismatch
The architecture only supports part of what the PRD or proposal promises.

## 8. Unsupported certainty
Words like 必然、全面、显著、可确保 are used without corresponding support.

## 9. Decision-ready illusion
The document sounds polished but still lacks the information needed for approval or execution.
