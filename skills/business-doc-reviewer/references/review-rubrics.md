# Review Rubrics

## Business Reviewer

Check:
- Is the problem worth solving?
- Is the value proposition clear?
- Is the recommendation supported?
- Is the document decision-oriented?

## Product Reviewer

Check:
- Are users and scenarios clear?
- Is scope controlled?
- Are requirements actionable?
- Are acceptance criteria present?

## Technical Reviewer

Check:
- Is the proposed design implementable?
- Are interfaces and modules coherent?
- Are dependencies visible?
- Are security/performance/scalability considerations real?

## Delivery Reviewer

Check:
- Is the timeline realistic?
- Are milestones useful?
- Are ownership and collaboration paths clear?
- Are rollout dependencies recognized?

## Risk Reviewer

Check:
- Are the biggest risks actually named?
- Are assumptions explicit?
- Are mitigation measures credible?
- Are compliance/data/operational concerns covered?

## Devil's Advocate

Check:
- Why should this not be approved yet?
- Which assumption, if false, breaks the entire document?
- What is being oversold?
- What is absent but should matter to a cautious decision-maker?
